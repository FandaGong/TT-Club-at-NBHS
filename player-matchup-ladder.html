The Game Day Flow

1

Check Your Rank

Open the site on your phone. If your row number changed after the last sort, your table might have changed. No manual promotions—the Elo is the boss.

2

Round Robin Play

Play everyone at your table in a Best of 3 sets. If you finish early, challenge someone from the same Division on an adjacent table.

3

Submit Scores

Record your wins/losses on the physical score sheet. Your Elo will be updated tonight via the Pilot script.

How to Rank Up

You don't wait for permission. Every win increases your Elo. When the rankings sort, if you've earned enough points, you will physically take the spot of the person above you. Win to climb.
