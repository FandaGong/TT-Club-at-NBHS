# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students who use the club site to check rankings, matchups, records, rules, and submit absence reports, plus club runners and admins who manage accounts, absence approvals, match entry, and season updates.

## Product Purpose

This site is the operational hub for the Normanhurst Boys Table Tennis Club. It keeps player information, session planning, absence reporting, and admin tools in one place so club operations stay fast, clear, and reliable.

## Brand Personality

Practical, trustworthy, school-appropriate.

The tone should feel like a well-run club system, not a flashy consumer app. It should be confident and organized, with enough personality to feel owned by the club.

## Anti-references

Generic SaaS dashboards, noisy admin panels, decorative gradients without purpose, and overly polished marketing-site aesthetics.

## Design Principles

Make the primary workflows obvious at a glance.

Prefer clear hierarchy over visual novelty.

Keep admin actions and student actions visually distinct but consistent.

Use motion and emphasis to support scanning, not to distract from it.

Preserve a friendly school-club feel while keeping the interface efficient.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, keyboard support, visible focus states, readable type, and reduced-motion-safe interactions.
